On planet Aether, the Warmroom Civilization has flourished for 
a millennium, sustained by the power of the Stellar Core.

The Randy Civilization, hailing from the depths of space, 
descended to plunder this energy—shattering the Stellar Core 
into fragments.

These scattered Stellar Shards gave birth to Feral Beasts, 
pushing the Warmroom Civilization to the edge of extinction.

Their chieftain rallied the entire clan and selected 
20 elite warriors known as Hunters, tasked with reclaiming 
the Stellar Shards.

Nineteen comrades fell in battle. You are the last Hunter. 
Fulfill the mission, avenge your fallen brothers and sisters, 
and save your civilization!
