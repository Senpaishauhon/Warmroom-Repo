Randy: Foolish humans, you dare defy me? 
The darkness swallows all, and your feeble 
strength means nothing. You can never defeat 
my mighty dark forces!

Hunter: The darkness may break our bodies, 
but it cannot break our hearts. Nineteen hunters’ souls 
walk with me. I am the last undead hero—their will 
fuels my every strike!

Hunter: You fall to fear and greed, calling 
darkness eternal. But courage is never alone; 
love transcends death. You take our lives, 
but not our unity!

Hunter: Even if I stand alone, 
I fight with every fallen hunter. 
Love and courage are the light that destroys the dark. 
Today, I shatter your death spell!
